Vision

The website aims to provide a valuable resource for individuals seeking guidance and support in nurturing strong and harmonious family relationships based on Islamic teachings.

Mission

1. To create a comprehensive online repository for collections of family tips and advice from prominent Islamic figures in Malaysia.

2. To provide a platform where individuals and families can access valuable insights, guidance, and practical tips on various aspects of family life from respected Islamic scholars and leaders in Malaysia.

3. To promote strong family values, foster harmonious relationships, and enhance the overall well-being of families within the Malaysian Muslim community.

4. To empower individuals with the wisdom and guidance needed to navigate the complexities of family life in accordance with Islamic teachings, ultimately contributing to the strengthening of family bonds and the promotion of a thriving and spiritually fulfilling family life.
